Why We Need This System

The Coronavirus Reporting System is crucial for tracking the spread of COVID-19 and providing quick diagnoses to those who may be infected.

Benefits include:

  • Early detection and response to outbreaks
  • Timely medical advice and treatment
  • Reducing the burden on healthcare systems
  • Helping in the fight against the pandemic

How It Works

Users can report their symptoms, and the system will provide a diagnosis based on the reported symptoms.

Users can also submit detailed reports for further medical attention and tracking.
